Meet Shlomi

Shlomi is the typical Tel Aviv guy, with his Afro and a Star of David, he's secretly listening to the iconic Diana in his Walkman.

Shlomi's Walkman

The Walkman was my way of discovering the world as a kid; I used to lie on the hammock and listen to the same CDs on loop, over and over again.

My vision for this was to capture the essence of a boiling hot day on a Tel Aviv beach.

It was essential for me to immerse the theater audience in that unique Tel Aviv atmosphere to make them feel the heat, taste the sea, and experience that distinct touch of cynicism that defines our city.
